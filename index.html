The Visionary

From Island Roots to Luxury Locs

Founded by Jay, Head Loc’d is more than a salon—it's a sanctuary born from a deep love for Afro-Caribbean hair culture. Jay’s journey began with a passion for the intricate art and spiritual significance of locs, inspired by her Jamaican heritage.

She has cultivated a space where traditional techniques meet modern luxury. Here, every client receives a bespoke experience, focusing on hair health, precision parting, and styles that are both timeless and boldly expressive. It's a commitment to elevating the art of the loctician to its most luxurious form.
